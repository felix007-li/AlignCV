## EN — Program Guide
### 1) What is AlignCV?
AlignCV is a resume & cover letter builder for North America with AI suggestions per section, 1:1 live preview, LinkedIn import, and localized templates (EN/FR‑CA; LATAM later). Pricing: **$0.99/14 days** or **Free 14‑day trial (card on file)**, **$2.99/month**, **$1.99 student one‑time**.

### 2) Commission & Cookie
- **30%** of the **first paid transaction only** (no recurring commission).  
- Cookie window **30 days**, **last‑click**. Reversals for refund/chargeback/fraud within **90 days**.

### 3) Tracking & Attribution
- UTM format:  
  `https://www.aligncv.com/?utm_source=aff&utm_medium={network}&utm_campaign={partner_id}&aff_sub={click_id}`
- Postback (server‑to‑server, first‑paid only):  
  `POST https://api.aligncv.com/affiliates/postback` JSON payload with `event, partner_id, click_id, amount, currency, tx, ts`.

### 4) Allowed Promotion
Content/review/comparison, YouTube/TikTok/IG creators, newsletters, campus orgs. **PPC**: non‑brand only (no “AlignCV/Align CV”). **Coupons**: official codes only.

### 5) Forbidden
Brand bidding, typo‑squatting, cookie stuffing, coupon‑injecting extensions, misleading claims, spam/malware, self‑purchase, fake reviews, incentive‑only clicks.

### 6) Creative
Logos (light/dark), product UI shots (editor/preview/StylePanel), template thumbnails. Landing: `/resume/templates`, `/resume/examples`, city×industry pages, `/pricing`.

### 7) Payout
Monthly **Net‑30**, **CAD**, PayPal/bank, threshold **$50**.

### 8) Campus Ambassador
Per‑campus pack: 10–20 redeem codes + slide deck + poster. CPS **30%** on first‑paid; monthly Top‑3 bonus **$50–100**.

### 9) Start
Apply → get partner id & test link → publish 1–3 assets → QA tracking → go live → monthly reporting.
