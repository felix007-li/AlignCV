# Software Engineer Resume Template

Create a professional software engineer resume with our modern template. Perfect for senior developers, team leads, and engineering managers.

## Key Features

- **ATS-Friendly**: Optimized for applicant tracking systems
- **Modern Design**: Clean, professional layout
- **Customizable**: Easy to adapt to your experience
- **Technical Focus**: Highlights skills and projects

## What to Include

1. **Professional Summary**: Brief overview of your experience and expertise
2. **Technical Skills**: Programming languages, frameworks, and tools
3. **Work Experience**: Focus on achievements and impact
4. **Education**: Degrees and relevant certifications
5. **Projects**: Notable projects with quantifiable results

## Example Bullet Points

- Led team of 5 developers in building scalable microservices architecture
- Improved application performance by 40% through code optimization
- Implemented CI/CD pipeline reducing deployment time by 60%
- Mentored junior developers and conducted code reviews

## Tips for Success

Use action verbs and quantify your achievements. Focus on the impact you made, not just your responsibilities.
